# Orion X Music Planet v2.0.0

## Release scope

Orion X Music Planet joins Orion Cinema with a dedicated Music Planet while keeping Cinema playback, subtitles, downloads, local media, Constellation and Windows integration intact.

## Music Planet

- Neutral Eclipse living scene with an audio-reactive Orb, accessible reduced-motion behavior and six-theme semantic tokens.
- YouTube Music catalog/discovery, YouTube Audio playback resolution, LRCLib lyrics, Spotify charts/import metadata and Local Library.
- Persistent dock with artwork, seek, previous/next, queue, lyrics, favorite, playlists, radio, volume, shuffle and repeat.
- SQLite-backed playlist folders, favorites, queue and listening history with non-destructive migrations.
- One audible playback owner: Cinema pauses Music and Music pauses Cinema while preserving the prior session.

## Shared reliability and identity

- New Orion X Music Planet icon and the tagline **A universe made to be felt.**
- Google sign-in uses a bundled Desktop OAuth client with PKCE. Users sign in with their own Google account and do not provide Cloud Console credentials.
- Google Drive backup includes portable Cinema and Music preferences, playlists, folders, favorites, queue and history. Credentials, signed URLs, caches and local paths remain private.
- The Orion-services indicator samples TMDB connectivity every 15 seconds and displays a rolling median.
- Battery alerts and their native notification clear when AC power returns; critical battery-paused downloads are resumed through the existing recovery flow.
- Cinema and Music Planet independently remember Expanded, Compact or Collapsed Rail sidebar modes, with keyboard access, migration and portable backup support.

## Validation

The release is gated by `npm.cmd run check`, the Electron suite and a fresh Windows NSIS/ZIP package build. Live Music, Cinema, downloader and Google sign-in smoke testing remain part of release acceptance on supported Windows systems.

## Known boundaries

Online music downloads, third-party runtime plugins, MCP control, podcasts, audiobooks and social collaboration are not part of v2.0. Music streaming is subject to provider availability; Local Library remains the guaranteed offline source.
