# Orion v1.0.9 — Cosmic Polish and Playback Continuity

v1.0.9 gives Orion a clearer visual identity while making streaming, downloads, and playback handoffs substantially more reliable.

## Highlights

- Added Orion's Cosmic Editorial styling, animated Orbit background, constellation sidebar interactions, refined light themes, and locally bundled font presets.
- Added discovery hubs for major streaming providers and franchise worlds while retaining movie/series, region, year, rating, and sorting filters.
- Expanded Downloads with clearer states, details, filtering, bulk actions, local playback, sidecar subtitles, and recovery guidance.
- Improved Library and Continue Watching presentation, progress details, sorting, search, history actions, and downloaded-media access.

## Playback continuity

- Unified embedded, mini, pop-out, and downloaded-media playback state.
- Preserved time, duration, pause, mute, volume, title, season, and episode context during handoffs.
- Reduced sidebar navigation blocking during automatic mini-player handoff from up to 3.5 seconds to a 160 ms snapshot deadline.
- Added a smoother shared-player transition into mini mode.
- Removed duplicate seek, volume, play, and mute controls from mini and pop-out chrome; the active player now owns playback controls.
- Corrected mini-player geometry to maintain a true 16:9 video surface, a 400 px default width, and usable resize constraints.
- Added restricted `orion-media://` local playback with range seeking, resume, subtitles, repair guidance, external open, and Show in folder.

## Ambient glow and appearance

- Online ambient sampling now captures the Electron guest webview rather than the surrounding renderer surface.
- Added a safe static palette fallback for protected or black video captures.
- Added smooth sampled-color shadows to embedded, mini, local, and pop-out playback surfaces.
- Ambient sampling pauses or slows when playback is inactive, minimized, hidden, power constrained, or Reduced Motion is enabled.
- Added animated counter-rotating Orbit rings with Calm, Balanced, Expressive, and Reduced Motion behavior.
- Added Orion, Sora, Outfit, Editorial, and Screen font presets, bundled locally without Google Fonts.
- Added circular spectral hover and active effects to sidebar navigation.

## Downloads and subtitles

- Preserved the working managed `yt-dlp` and ffmpeg strategy with captured request context, protected-HLS proxy fallback, resumable fragments, retries, and organized Movie/Series folders.
- Improved stream candidate scoping and detection for HLS, extensionless manifests, DASH, and direct video responses.
- Added actionable waiting, expired, unsupported, browser-only, and DRM states instead of an indefinite detection message.
- Added subtitle selection and sidecar downloads through user-configured SubDL or Wyzie credentials.
- Added local playback, open externally, Show in folder, pause/resume, retry, cancel, diagnostics, and bulk queue actions.

## Reliability fixes

- Fixed webview operations occurring before `dom-ready`.
- Fixed downloader tool installation and AllManga preload bindings after the architecture refactor.
- Fixed frameless-window controls, sidebar pin/unpin, pop-out ownership, mini-player audio/state restoration, and persistent loading overlays.
- Fixed Play in Orion incorrectly invoking folder reveal behavior.
- Preserved v1.0.7/v1.0.8 settings, downloads, history, progress, secure provider keys, and interrupted jobs through migrations.
- Added regression coverage for webview lifecycle, guest ambient sampling, mini-player controls and geometry, stalled playback snapshots, IPC compatibility, downloader records, proxy rewriting, and local media ranges.

## Configuration and security

- Orion uses its bundled TMDB read token when available; users do not need to configure TMDB for normal use.
- SubDL and Wyzie credentials remain optional per-user settings protected through Electron secure storage.
- Cookies, captured request headers, provider credentials, executable paths, and unrestricted proxy URLs remain outside the renderer.
- DRM circumvention is not supported. Download or play only content you are authorized to access.

## Validation

- Node and renderer unit/integration suites.
- Source-size, renderer-binding, IPC compatibility, secret, and dependency-cycle checks.
- Production Vite build and Windows NSIS/ZIP packaging.
- Live streaming and downloader smoke testing performed during the v1.0.9 stabilization cycle.
